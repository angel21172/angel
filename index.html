Shipping Options
Option 1 — Solo Order
Direct Shipping to PH Address
ISF is already included in your total upfront — one payment covers everything. Estimated delivery is 7–10 days via air freight (barring delays).

This is the best option for large items, since ISF in consolidation is based on actual or volumetric weight of the parcel — the bigger the packaging, the higher the ISF. Air freight means faster ETA to your address.But you may also choose consolidation shipping if you don't want direct air freight shipment.
Option 2 — Consolidation Shipping
Batch Shipment — Two Payments
Items ship together at the end of every month.

1st Payment: Item price only — paid before purchase.
2nd Payment: ISF + local shipping fee — collected once your item arrives in the Philippines.

If you're not ready to ship right away, your item can be held in the consolidation warehouse for up to 20 days.
